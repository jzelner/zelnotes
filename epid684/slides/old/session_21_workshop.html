Agenda

Instructions for the workshop

  1. Read through the document shared with you by your partner.
  • If you have questions about their feedback, make a comment in the document.
  1. In groups:
  • Decide who will go first and have that person do a brief overview of their feedback.
  • Discuss the feedback, being sure to take notes either in the review document or another one.
  • Switch, rinse, repeat
